Recommended order for creating entity records:

1. DataType  (Needed for ElementDefinition and ElementValue)
2. MetadataSchemaVersion  (Needed for ElementDefinition)
3. AssetType  (Needed for Asset)
4. Customer  (Needed for Sale)
5. Inventory  (Needed for Asset)
6. ElementDefinition  (Depends on DataType and MetadataSchemaVersion)
7. Asset  (Depends on AssetType and Inventory)
8. Sale  (Depends on Customer and Asset)
9. ElementValue  (Depends on Asset, ElementDefinition, and DataType)

This order ensures all required parent records exist before creating child records that reference them.
